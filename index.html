Not convinced yet?

Let's take a simple scenario: You are driving on a peaceful country-side road and suddenly you hear an odd sound around the front right side of the car. You are right in the middle of nowhere and you have no idea what could cause the problem. The problem may be really simple, like a broken sensor, or fatal to your car, like a broken bearing. It would be very expensive to call assistance. Here we enter into the ecuation, offering you immediate and accurate car fault diagnosis. Convinced?

SHORT HISTORY

Imagined in 2018 by Andrei Porumbescu for his final license project,
© PORUMBESCU Diagnosis is a web app that uses machine learning techniques in order to diagnose the faults of a car. A new user has to create an account in order to use the app. A Mongo database is used to store the users and their cars. Enjoy using it!
